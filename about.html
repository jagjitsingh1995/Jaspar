Our Story

Built on a Foundation of Quality

Founded in 2009, Jaspar Fabrication LTD began as a small workshop with a big vision: to deliver metal fabrication that stands the test of time. What started as a one-man operation has grown into a trusted name in the industry.

Over the years, we've refined our craft, invested in cutting-edge equipment, and assembled a team of skilled fabricators who share our passion for precision. Every railing, gate, and fence we create reflects our commitment to excellence.

Today, we serve residential and commercial clients across the region, turning raw steel and aluminium into functional works of art that enhance properties and stand the test of time.
